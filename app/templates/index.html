lncHUB2

Functional predictions of human long non-coding RNAs based on lncRNA-gene co-expression correlations.

A long non-coding RNA (lncRNA) is a transcript with more than 200 nucleotides that is not translated into protein. Based on gene-gene co-expression correlations created from Recount3's processed RNA-seq samples, we present 15,862 landing pages for long non-coding RNAs that include median expression across tissues and cell lines, predicted biological functions, pathway membership, and predicted small molecules that may regulate their expression.

For example HOTAIR, MALAT1, NEAT1
